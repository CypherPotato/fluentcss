Flyout

Flyouts are small modals that are displayed under the user mouse, which tracks attention and requires input to handle actions.

A button with a flyout

All items will be removed. Do you want to continue? 


                    
                        <div class="win-flyout" id="myFlyout">
                            <p>
                                All items will be removed. Do you want to continue?
                            </p>
                            <button class="win-button" onclick="alert('Cart empty.')">
                                Yes, empty my cart
                            </button>
                        </div>
                        <button onclick="win.flyout('#myFlyout')" class="win-button">
                            Empty cart
                        </button>
