Expanders

An expander is a component that holds a title and content, and can be minimized or maximized to hold multiple contents of the same type.

Simple expander

What languages is FluentCSS written in?
Only CSS and vanilla Javascript! 

                    
                        <details class="win-expander">
                            <summary>
                                What languages is FluentCSS written in?
                            </summary>
                            <div>
                                Only SCSS and vanilla Javascript!
                            </div>
                        </details>

                        <details class="win-expander">
