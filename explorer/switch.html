Switchs and tabs

A switchable control can programmatically toggle the visibility of another component.

Switcher using radio buttons

This is the blue box!
This is the red box!
This is the green box! 

                    
                        <div class="win-stack">
                            <label class="win-check">
                                <input type="radio" name="boxes" onclick="win.switch('#blueBox')" checked>
                                Blue
                            </label>
                            <label class="win-check">
                                <input type="radio" name="boxes" onclick="win.switch('#redBox')">
                                Red
                            </label>
                            <label class="win-check">
                                <input type="radio" name="boxes" onclick="win.switch('#greenBox')">
                                Green
                            </label>
                        </div>
                        <div class="switch-group-container" style="width: 200px; height: 100px;">
                            <div class="win-depth-1" id="blueBox" data-switch-group="boxes" open>
                                This is the blue box!
                            </div>
                            <div class="win-depth-1" id="redBox" data-switch-group="boxes">
                                This is the red box!
                            </div>
                            <div class="win-depth-1" id="greenBox" data-switch-group="boxes">
                                This is the green box!
                            </div>
                        </div>

Pivot using switch

You're reading all the mails now

On the unread tab...

And flagged tab! 


                    
                        <div class="win-pivot">
                            <label class="pivot-item" onclick="win.switch('#all')">
                                <input type="radio" checked name="__pivot_group">
                                All
                            </label>
                            <label class="pivot-item" onclick="win.switch('#unread')">
                                <input type="radio" name="__pivot_group">
                                Unread
                            </label>
                            <label class="pivot-item" onclick="win.switch('#flagged')">
                                <input type="radio" name="__pivot_group">
                                Flagged
                            </label>
                        </div>
                        <div class="switch-group-container horizontal" style="width: 100%; height: 200px;">
                            <div data-switch-group="__group_name" id="all" open>
                                <p>
                                    You're reading all the mails now
                                </p>
                            </div>
                            <div data-switch-group="__group_name" id="unread">
                                <p>
                                    On the unread tab...
                                </p>
                            </div>
                            <div data-switch-group="__group_name" id="flagged">
                                <p>
                                    And flagged tab!
                                </p>
                            </div>
                        </div>
